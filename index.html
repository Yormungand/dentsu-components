About Us

Dentsu Data Artist Mongol LLC was established as a subsidiary of Data Artist Inc. in June 2018, in Ulaanbaatar Mongolia. Currently we are operating with around 50 Mongolian youths, from which 30 are located in Mongolia. Company office is located at Central Tower, one of the most prestigious office buildings in Ulaanbaatar.

As a member of Dentsu Japan Network we have been providing digital marketing services to Dentsu Group. During three years since the establishment, we have successfully delivered over 80 projects, in which we`ve developed AI and Web systems together with Data analysis.

During the period we have recruited over 100 Engineers and Data analysts, who are now successfully pursuing their dreams in Global market, and in Mongolian Technology startup Companies. Also, we collaborate with other local Technology Companies with whom we share same values and belong to same Machine Learning Community.

Since the third year of the operation, we have started to accomplish our annual sales budget, and now are eager to expand our business to Dentsu International.
